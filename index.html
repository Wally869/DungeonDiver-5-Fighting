Dungeon Diver - Fighting

About

Dungeon Diver is a learning project for GameDevelopment in Unity.
This page showcases the creation of a simple Fighting system and a basic AI in a RPG Game.
Step on the Cardboard-like plate to initiate a fight. Notifications of damage and healing can be seen in the Window Console (Ctrl+Shift+J).
